Project Brief

Engage in the design process to research, conceptualise, design, test and produce a three dimensional design product.

Research, plan, collaborate, design, test and produce a three dimensional design product and produce a digital mock-up/photographic spread of the product.

Research

With the current COVID pandemic, I could not think any better than designing face mask packaging for family as bundle pack.
After exploration and inspiration from the internet, I designed a logo for the product.

Logo design

Product Design

Elements and Principles of design

Application of elements and principles of design are:

Colour: Choice of colour which is really important to please the customers as well as to create visually attractive with a good contrast colour palette.
Negative space: as important as colour, it is also very crucial to play with white space in design and blend of white space design best suited my packaging.
Randomness: no specific guidelines and margins, my package design simply placed where it best suited.

Colour theory/management

Since my product is a face mask, I chose to pick the primary colour as the colour of mask which was steel blue and then created the blue theme. I tried to generate the colour theme with the help of Adobe Colour Palette generator.

Typographic Approach

Since my product is to do with hygiene and safety, it required to be decent and vigilant feel. So, I decided to choose “PT Sans” font family throughout all my package design. It didn’t require me to font pair since this font offered me with its variability.

Packaging Design

After designing in Adobe illustrator and InDesign, the next process was to print but before that, I tried to print in normal A4 and made a mock-up to check if there is anything to be corrected. After fixing all the errors and modification, then only final design was sent to print.

Critical and creative thinking strategies

This project helped me to think creatively in 3D perspective as well as work with die-cut and fold lines which otherwise is not achievable through standard digital designs.

At initial stage, it was a challenge to think what product and brand works hand-in-hand to be presented as a whole to be packaged that best suits the customer who will use those products.
Next, drafting several concepts with physical measurements and best judgement to bring out the design into practical use-ability as a real product was a real challenge.
